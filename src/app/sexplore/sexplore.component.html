What is the Sexplore App all about?
The idea behind Vanilla4Kink is to help couples who are sexual opposites overcome their inhibitions and better their sex lives. Our Sexplore App is focused on providing ideas and experiences that real-life couples have contributed to provide you with real-life experiences coupled with research.

Who are these "real-life" couples?
Over the course of about year, we've sought out and gathered a small community or "panel" of couples, both Vanilla and Kinks, to discuss, create, and provide feedback and stories on the several sex acts and fetishes they have experience with. These couples have been specifically identified through a series of questionnaires and interviews to help ensure they have:
  • A Healthy Loving Relationship
  • Strong Sexual Differences between partners (a Vanilla partner and a Kink partner)
  • An active and involved sex life
  • Had to overcome sexual inhibitions.
  • Good dialogue and open communication
We wanted to be "picky" to ensure the content they provide is as clean and as useful as possible.

What kind of research was done?
We've done quite a bit of due diligence through
  • Interviews with therapists
  • Insights from self-help books and blogs
  • Interviews with couples who have had similar experiences
Full disclosure, no one at V4K is a licensed therapist. We wanted our approach to be a little less formal and more directed at couples helping couples. We feel that other couples experiences take you outside of your relationship and better demonstrate chemistry, what's possible, and what possible expectations or outcomes can be... something we feel most sex blogs and articles fail miserably at. It's easier to discuss the "why" and "how" but its more difficult to paint a picture you can specifically relate to. Let's face it- have you really ever had a life-changing premonition from reading Cosmo?

How it works
Simply configure the settings to Sexplore fetish content that has been provided by Kinks who enjoy these sex acts. The "Threshold" setting allows you filter content that may be a "little too much" or a "little too little" for you at this point in your journey. Setting the "Threshold" to "Low" will provide you with some basics about each Fetish, Category, or Sex Act and how to "get your feet wet". Setting the "Threshold" to "Medium" or "High" will provide you with activities and suggestions to try. Content is frequently added so be sure to check back later if you don't see the fetish you are interested in or have read through all the content for a particular fetish.

Always Remember!! The content delivered by this app are suggestions meant to give you ideas and help you understand. While these suggestions are popular, it is no substitute for communication between you and your Kink. You and you Kink are your own Sexplore app.
